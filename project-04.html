Project Overview

Two models, one vision

This project showcases my journey in managing diverse business models — ranging from digital system distribution to the creative production of handmade accessories. It demonstrates my ability to handle different market demands, from digital networking to physical product craftsmanship.

On one side, a fully digital operation built around database distribution and a dropshipping workflow designed to generate passive income and grow a network of resellers. On the other, a hands-on creative studio where every phone strap and bracelet is hand-assembled from scratch using carefully sourced beads and materials.

Managing both simultaneously sharpened my skills in digital organization, visual marketing, customer communication, and quality control — all executed independently through a smartphone and creative workspace.

Problem & Solution

The Challenge & The Fix

⚖️ The Challenge

Two very different businesses — running at the same time

Managing two completely opposing business models simultaneously presented a real operational conflict. The digital side demanded high-level digital communication, networking strategy, and system-building — fast-paced and largely invisible. The creative side required manual attention, physical inventory management, and aesthetic quality control for every single handmade piece. Without a clear separation of processes, both risked falling behind.

🎯 The Solution

Dual-Strategy Management — one system for each world

I implemented a Dual-Strategy Management approach, treating each business with its own dedicated workflow. For the digital business, I built a "User-Join System" and streamlined dropshipping pipeline to ensure passive income and steady network growth. For the creative side, I managed the full production line — from sourcing beads and materials to hand-assembling each unique phone strap and bracelet — ensuring every piece met high-quality aesthetic standards before being marketed on social media.

Operations Methodology

How I Manage

01
Digital System Setup & Reseller Onboarding
Establish the User-Join System infrastructure for the digital business — setting up Telegram broadcasts, onboarding flows for new resellers, and building a network that runs with minimal daily intervention.
02
Handmade Production & Quality Control
Source beads and craft materials, then hand-assemble each phone strap and bracelet individually. Every finished piece is quality-checked for consistency, durability, and aesthetic standard before being photographed for listing.
03
Visual Marketing & Content Creation
Design promotional content in Canva for both businesses — maintaining distinct visual identities. The digital business uses bold, CTA-driven graphics; the handmade brand uses soft, lifestyle-style product photography edited in Lightroom Mobile.
04
Order Tracking, Customer Service & Review
Manage all incoming orders across both businesses via DM and Telegram. Track payments, shipping, and customer data in spreadsheets. Review weekly performance — what sold, what didn't, and how to adjust the next campaign.
